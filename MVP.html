The 10 Trading Archetypes Diagnostic Survey

1. Time Horizon: What is your preferred holding period for a successful trade?

2. Risk Capital: How much of your trading capital are you willing to put at risk on a single trade idea?

3. Analysis Type: What is your primary focus when deciding to make a trade?

4. Primary Goal: What is the main reason you trade/invest?

5. Emotion and Discipline: How do you react to a trade moving 20% against you shortly after you put it on?

6. Strategy Preference: Which trade strategy appeals most to you?

7. Information Source: Which type of market information do you find most compelling?

8. Handling Success: If a trade is up 100% in a week, what do you do?

9. Market Environment: Which market condition do you believe offers the best opportunities?

10. The Flaw: Which of the following describes your biggest mental struggle in trading?
